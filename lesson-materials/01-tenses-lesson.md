## 📖 PART 2: Tense Formulas & Signal Words (15 minutes)

### A. PAST TENSES

#### 🔵 PAST SIMPLE
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + V2 (past form) |
| **Negative** | Subject + did not + V1 |
| **Question** | Did + subject + V1? |

**Signal Words:** `yesterday`, `last week/month/year`, `ago`, `in 2010`, `when I was young`

**Examples:**
- I **visited** Paris last summer. ✓
- She **didn't call** me yesterday. ✓
- **Did** you **see** the movie? ✓

---

#### 🔵 PAST CONTINUOUS
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + was/were + V-ing |
| **Negative** | Subject + was/were + not + V-ing |
| **Question** | Was/Were + subject + V-ing? |

**Signal Words:** `while`, `when`, `at that moment`, `at 5 PM yesterday`, `all day`

**Examples:**
- I **was reading** when she arrived. ✓
- They **were playing** football at 5 PM. ✓
- **Were** you **sleeping** when I called? ✓

---

#### 🔵 PAST PERFECT
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + had + V3 (past participle) |
| **Negative** | Subject + had not + V3 |
| **Question** | Had + subject + V3? |

**Signal Words:** `before`, `after`, `already`, `by the time`, `when`, `never... until`

**Examples:**
- I **had finished** dinner before he came. ✓
- She **had never seen** snow until then. ✓
- **Had** you **met** him before the party? ✓

> ⚠️ **KEY RULE:** Use Past Perfect for the EARLIER event, Past Simple for the LATER event.
> 
> `When I arrived (LATER), the movie had already started (EARLIER).`

---

### B. PRESENT TENSES

#### 🟢 PRESENT SIMPLE
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + V1 (he/she/it + V1+s) |
| **Negative** | Subject + do/does not + V1 |
| **Question** | Do/Does + subject + V1? |

**Signal Words:** `always`, `usually`, `often`, `sometimes`, `never`, `every day/week`

**Examples:**
- I **drink** coffee every morning. ✓
- The sun **rises** in the east. ✓
- **Does** she **work** on Saturdays? ✓

---

#### 🟢 PRESENT CONTINUOUS
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + am/is/are + V-ing |
| **Negative** | Subject + am/is/are + not + V-ing |
| **Question** | Am/Is/Are + subject + V-ing? |

**Signal Words:** `now`, `at the moment`, `currently`, `right now`, `today`, `this week`

**Examples:**
- I **am studying** English right now. ✓
- She **is working** from home today. ✓
- **Are** you **listening** to me? ✓

---

#### 🟢 PRESENT PERFECT
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + have/has + V3 |
| **Negative** | Subject + have/has + not + V3 |
| **Question** | Have/Has + subject + V3? |

**Signal Words:** `already`, `just`, `yet`, `ever`, `never`, `since`, `for`, `so far`, `recently`

**Examples:**
- I **have visited** Japan twice. ✓
- She **has lived** here since 2015. ✓
- **Have** you **ever eaten** sushi? ✓

> ⚠️ **CRITICAL DIFFERENCE:**
> - **Past Simple** = finished time → *I saw him YESTERDAY.*
> - **Present Perfect** = unfinished time or no time → *I have seen that movie (before/ever).*

---

### C. FUTURE TENSES

#### 🟣 FUTURE SIMPLE
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + will + V1 |
| **Negative** | Subject + will not (won't) + V1 |
| **Question** | Will + subject + V1? |

**Signal Words:** `tomorrow`, `next week`, `soon`, `in the future`, `probably`

**Alternative:** `be going to` for plans → *I'm going to travel next month.*

---

#### 🟣 FUTURE CONTINUOUS
| Component | Formula |
|-----------|---------|
| **Affirmative** | Subject + will be + V-ing |

**Signal Words:** `at this time tomorrow`, `next week at 3 PM`, `when you arrive`

**Example:** *This time next year, I **will be living** in London.*

## ✍️ PART 3: Exercises (20 minutes)

### Exercise 1: Tense Contrast Stories

**Instructions:** Fill in the blanks with the correct form of the verb in parentheses.

---

#### Story A: "The Interview" 

Yesterday, I _____________ (go) to a job interview. Before I _____________ (arrive), I _____________ (prepare) for three days. When the interviewer asked me about my experience, I told her that I _____________ (work) in marketing for five years.

I _____________ (not/hear) back from them yet. I _____________ (wait) for their call since last week. Tomorrow, I _____________ (call) them if I _____________ (not/receive) any news by then.

---

#### Story B: "The Journey"

Last summer, my family _____________ (decide) to go on a road trip. We _____________ (never/travel) across the country before, so we were very excited. My father _____________ (drive) for ten hours before we _____________ (reach) our first destination.

Right now, I _____________ (look) at the photos from that trip. I _____________ (already/show) them to all my friends. Next summer, we _____________ (plan) to visit a new country.

---

#### Story C: "The Project"

I _____________ (work) on this project since Monday. Before I _____________ (start), I _____________ (read) all the instructions carefully. Yesterday, I _____________ (finish) the first section, but I _____________ (not/complete) the second one yet.

My boss _____________ (ask) me about my progress every day. Right now, I _____________ (try) to solve a difficult problem. I hope I _____________ (finish) everything by Friday.

---

### Exercise 1: ANSWER KEY

#### Story A: "The Interview"
- Yesterday, I **went** (go) to a job interview.
- Before I **arrived** (arrive), I **had prepared** (prepare) for three days.
- When the interviewer asked me about my experience, I told her that I **had worked** (work) in marketing for five years.
- I **haven't heard** (not/hear) back from them yet.
- I **have been waiting** (wait) for their call since last week.
- Tomorrow, I **will call** (call) them if I **haven't received / don't receive** (not/receive) any news by then.

#### Story B: "The Journey"
- Last summer, my family **decided** (decide) to go on a road trip.
- We **had never traveled** (never/travel) across the country before, so we were very excited.
- My father **had driven / drove** (drive) for ten hours before we **reached** (reach) our first destination.
- Right now, I **am looking** (look) at the photos from that trip.
- I **have already shown** (already/show) them to all my friends.
- Next summer, we **are planning / plan** (plan) to visit a new country.

#### Story C: "The Project"
- I **have been working** (work) on this project since Monday.
- Before I **started** (start), I **had read** (read) all the instructions carefully.
- Yesterday, I **finished** (finish) the first section, but I **haven't completed** (not/complete) the second one yet.
- My boss **asks / has been asking** (ask) me about my progress every day.
- Right now, I **am trying** (try) to solve a difficult problem.
- I hope I **will finish / will have finished** (finish) everything by Friday.

**Circle the correct answer:**

1. I _____________ to Paris three times.
   - a) went
   - b) have been
   - c) had been

2. When I arrived at the station, the train _____________.
   - a) already left
   - b) has already left
   - c) had already left

3. She _____________ in this company since 2018.
   - a) worked
   - b) has worked
   - c) is working

4. I _____________ TV when the phone rang.
   - a) watched
   - b) was watching
   - c) have watched

5. By the time you arrive, I _____________ cooking dinner.
   - a) will finish
   - b) will have finished
   - c) am finishing

6. What _____________ at 8 PM yesterday?
   - a) did you do
   - b) were you doing
   - c) have you done

7. I _____________ him since we were children.
   - a) knew
   - b) have known
   - c) am knowing

8. She _____________ to London next week.
   - a) flies
   - b) is flying
   - c) Both a and b are correct

9. I _____________ never _____________ sushi before I went to Japan.
   - a) have... eaten
   - b) had... eaten
   - c) did... eat

10. Look! It _____________ outside.
    - a) rains
    - b) is raining
    - c) has rained

---

### Exercise 2: ANSWER KEY

1. **b) have been** - experience, no specific time
2. **c) had already left** - earlier past action
3. **b) has worked** - since + Present Perfect
4. **b) was watching** - interrupted action
5. **b) will have finished** - completed before future point
6. **b) were you doing** - action in progress at past time
7. **b) have known** - since + Present Perfect
8. **c) Both a and b are correct** - scheduled future
9. **b) had... eaten** - before another past action
10. **b) is raining** - happening now

   > _______________________________________________

2. When I was young, I have played football every day.
   > _______________________________________________

3. She is living in Istanbul since 2010.
   > _______________________________________________

4. I already finished my homework.
   > _______________________________________________

   > _______________________________________________

   > _______________________________________________

   > _______________________________________________

   > _______________________________________________

---

### Exercise 3: ANSWER KEY

1. I **saw** him yesterday. *(Past Simple with specific past time)*
2. When I was young, I **played** football every day. *(Past Simple for past habits)*
3. She **has been living / has lived** in Istanbul since 2010. *(Present Perfect with "since")*
4. I **have** already finished my homework. *(Present Perfect with "already")*
5. **Have** you ever **visited** London? *(Present Perfect with "ever")*
6. I **have known** him for many years. *("know" is a state verb, not continuous)*
7. By the time he arrived, the meeting **had** already started. *(Past Perfect for earlier action)*
8. She usually **drinks** coffee in the morning. *(Present Simple for habits)*

## 🗣️ PART 4: Speaking Activity - Life Timeline (15 minutes)

### Instructions for Student:

Think about **5 important events** in your life. Describe them using the following structure:

1. **When it happened** (Past Simple)
2. **What you had done before** (Past Perfect)
3. **How it has affected you until now** (Present Perfect)
4. **What you are doing related to it now** (Present Continuous)
5. **What you will do in the future** (Future)

### Example:

> "I **started** learning English when I was 12 years old. Before that, I **had never studied** any foreign language. Since then, I **have learned** many things and my skills **have improved** a lot. Right now, I **am preparing** for the IELTS exam. Next year, I **will apply** to universities abroad."

### Student's Events to Describe:

| Event | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 1. Education milestone | (starting school, graduation, etc.) |
| 2. Travel experience | (a trip or journey) |
| 3. Personal achievement | (something you're proud of) |
| 4. Family event | (birthday, wedding, etc.) |
| 5. Career/Study decision | (job, course, etc.) |

### Teacher Notes:
- Listen for correct tense usage
- Note down errors for correction after speaking
- Encourage use of signal words
- Ask follow-up questions to elicit more tenses

## 📊 PART 5: Quick Reference Summary

### When to Use Each Tense:

| Tense | Use When... | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Past Simple | Action finished in the past | *I ate pizza yesterday.* |
| Past Continuous | Action in progress at past time | *I was eating when you called.* |
| Past Perfect | Action before another past action | *I had eaten before you came.* |
| Present Simple | Habits, facts, routines | *I eat breakfast every day.* |
| Present Continuous | Happening now / temporary | *I am eating right now.* |
| Present Perfect | Past → Now connection | *I have eaten sushi before.* |
| Future Simple | Predictions, promises | *I will eat later.* |
| Future Continuous | In progress at future time | *I will be eating at 7 PM.* |
